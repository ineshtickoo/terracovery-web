The Problem

Desertification is a broad term that incorporates a variety of processes, other concepts, drivers, and solutions, as well as people from varied backgrounds and interests (farmers, scientists, policymakers, and so on). With all of their (internal) feedback and interrelationships, these concerns are still not fully understood. As a result, it is impossible to provide a comprehensive overview of desertification and its consequences.

TerraCovery aims to Identify, Cure and Prevent desertification and soil degradation around the world by providing legible solutions that are the the best fit for the topography of that area. Our services use high-end deep learning techniques to identify and predict potential desertification areas and help curb this problem before any major effects happen on the soil.
